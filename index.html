why?

AICTE IdeaLab

  • The AICTE IDEA Lab promotes innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among students in technical education institutions.

  • Key objectives include encouraging innovation, fostering entrepreneurial development, enhancing technical and entrepreneurial skills, bridging academic-industry collaboration, offering incubation and support, networking, and protecting intellectual property.

  • The lab's mission and objectives may evolve over time.

Know Us More

  • What is IdeaLab?

    IdeaLab is a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas and get them implemented.

  • How to join IdeaLab?

    Students can join IdeaLab by registering themselves in the website.

  • What are the benefits of joining IdeaLab?

    Students can get their ideas implemented and also get a chance to work with the industry experts.

  • How to get my idea implemented?

    Students can get their ideas implemented by submitting their ideas in the website.
